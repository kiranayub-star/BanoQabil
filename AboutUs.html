Our Vision

Our vision at Bano Qabil is to empower the youth of Pakistan by providing accessible, high-quality education and skill development opportunities that foster self-reliance, innovation, and career readiness. We aim to create a generation of skilled, confident, and responsible individuals who can contribute meaningfully to society and the nationâ€™s progress.

Our Mission

The mission of Bano Qabil is to equip young people with practical skills, digital literacy, and professional knowledge through accessible and high-quality training programs. We strive to empower youth to achieve self-reliance, secure meaningful employment, and build a brighter future while fostering innovation, confidence, and social responsibility.

Why Choose BanoQabil

Bano Qabil is a trusted platform for youth empowerment through education and skill development. We offer high-quality, free practical training in technical, digital, and professional skills that prepare students for real-world careers.

Our Pioneers

Hafiz Naeem Ur Rehman

Patron-in-Chief, Bano Qabil Program

Hafiz Naeem Ur Rehman is a Pakistani civil engineer, politician, and the current Ameer of Jamaat-e-Islami (JI) Pakistan. Known for his work in social activism, urban development, and IT education (Bano Qabil), he has a strong background in student politics and previously led the JI Karachi chapter.

Mr. Naveed Ali Baig

Chairman Bano Qabil Pakistan

Naveed Ali Baig is a veteran Pakistani technology leader and corporate strategist with over 30 years of experience, currently serving as CEO of Innovative Pvt. Ltd. and in leadership roles with Alkhidmat Foundation and Bano Qabil. He focuses on IT services, digital transformation, and youth empowerment.

Farooq Kamlani

Director Bano Qabil

Farooq Kamalani is the Director of Bano Qabil, a visionary educational movement dedicated to empowering youth through free, high-quality technical and professional training. With a strong commitment to social development, he plays a key role in guiding programs that equip students with practical skills in IT, digital technologies, and career development, helping them build a brighter and self-reliant future.
