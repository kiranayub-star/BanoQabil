Harrapa Campus Visit

Supervisor Alkhidmat Foundation District Sahiwal, Mr. Tayyab Mahmood Baloch, along with General Secretary Alkhidmat Foundation District Sahiwal Mr. Saifullah Khalid and Master Mazhar Hussain, visited Bano Qabil Harappa. On this occasion, they conducted a detailed review of the ongoing classes, appreciated the students’ attendance, the teaching system, and overall educational activities.

Meeting at Alkhidmat Head Office

A key meeting was held at the Alkhidmat Head Office in Lahore for a comprehensive review and future planning of Bano Qabil Pakistan, in which the Alkhidmat Foundation Central Punjab team participated. The meeting was chaired by Shahid Iqbal (CEO, Bano Qabil), who conducted a detailed review of ongoing progress, analyzed the program’s performance, and presented important strategic points for the next phase.
